Greek Salad

Description

This Greek salad uses fresh vegetables that keep a firm texture. The mix of tomatoes, cucumbers, and onions gives a clean taste. The olives and feta add a steady salty note.

The dressing stays light and does not cover the flavor of the vegetables. It works as a side dish or a quick meal.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes chopped
  • Cucumbers chopped
  • Red onion sliced
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese cubed
  • Olive oil
  • Red wine vinegar
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Add tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, and olives to a bowl.
  2. Place the feta on top.
  3. Mix olive oil, vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the salad.
  5. Toss and serve.
